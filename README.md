# 📡 Telco Customer Churn Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer churn behavior and provide actionable business insights.

The dashboard enables users to explore churn trends across customer demographics, contract types, payment methods, internet services, and tenure groups.

- Executive KPI Cards

---

## 📌 Key Business Insights

- Fiber Optic customers recorded the highest churn.
- Month-to-Month contracts showed the highest churn rate.
- Electronic Check was the most common payment method among churned customers.
- Customers with shorter tenure were more likely to churn.
- Average Monthly Charges were higher for churned customers.
